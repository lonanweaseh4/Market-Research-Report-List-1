<p><strong>The Evaporative Industrial Cooling Tower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cooling Tower</li><li>Closed Cooling Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The evaporative industrial cooling tower market comprises two main types: open cooling towers and closed cooling towers. Open cooling towers allow ambient air to contact water, promoting evaporation and cooling. They are efficient but may have concerns regarding water quality and drift. In contrast, closed cooling towers circulate water through closed coils, minimizing water exposure to the environment and reducing contamination risks. This design offers better efficiency in sensitive applications, though it typically has higher installation and maintenance costs.</p></p>

<p><p>The Hot Cast Urethane Elastomer market is primarily categorized into three types based on the isocyanate used: TDI-based, MDI-based, and Others. TDI-based elastomers, derived from toluene diisocyanate, offer high flexibility and are commonly used in various applications such as automotive and industrial components. MDI-based elastomers, sourced from methylene diphenyl diisocyanate, provide greater hardness, abrasion resistance, and thermal stability, making them suitable for demanding applications. "Others" includes alternative formulations that cater to specific niche requirements, enhancing versatility in various industries.</p></p>

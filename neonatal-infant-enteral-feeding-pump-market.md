<p><p>The Neonatal Infant Enteral Feeding Pump market is divided into two main types: Continuous Feeding Type and Cycled Feeding Type. Continuous Feeding Pumps deliver a constant flow of nutrition over an extended period, ensuring a steady intake for vulnerable neonates. In contrast, Cycled Feeding Pumps dispense nutrition at set intervals, allowing for breaks between feedings, which can mimic natural eating patterns. Both types serve critical roles in supporting the growth and health of premature or ill infants requiring specialized care.</p></p>

<p><strong>The HDPE Granule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoclave Process</li><li>Tubular Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE granule market is primarily segmented into two production processes: the autoclave process and the tubular process. The autoclave process involves high-pressure reactors where ethylene is polymerized, resulting in a broad molecular weight distribution and enhanced properties suitable for various applications. In contrast, the tubular process employs continuous flow through a tubular reactor, offering higher productivity and lower operational costs. Both methods deliver distinct characteristics in HDPE granules, catering to diverse industrial needs, including packaging, containers, and piping systems.</p></p>

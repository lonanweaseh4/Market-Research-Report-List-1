<p><p>The Tooth Positioner Market is categorized into Non-Extraction and Extraction Positioners. Non-Extraction Positioners are designed for orthodontic treatments where teeth can be realigned without the need for extractions, aiding in gentle adjustments. In contrast, Extraction Positioners are used when tooth removal is necessary, facilitating the movement of remaining teeth to fill gaps and achieve proper alignment. Both types play crucial roles in orthodontics, enhancing aesthetic outcomes and improving oral function by ensuring correct dental positioning.</p></p>

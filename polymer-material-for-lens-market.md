<p><p>The polymer materials for the lens market include several types, each with unique properties. CR-39 is a lightweight, impact-resistant plastic commonly used in prescription lenses. Polycarbonate (PC) is known for its superior impact resistance and UV protection, making it ideal for safety eyewear. Cyclo Olefin Copolymer (COC) offers excellent optical clarity and moisture resistance. Poly(Methyl Methacrylate) (PMMA, Acrylic) provides high transparency and scratch resistance but is less impact-resistant. Polystyrene is lightweight and cost-effective but has lower durability in comparison.</p></p>

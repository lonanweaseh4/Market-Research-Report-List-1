<p><p>The polyfilm market consists of various types based on resin properties and applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and resilience, making it ideal for packaging. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) provides strength and stretchability. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for high tensile strength. BoPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) is preferred for its clarity and barrier properties. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) features a soft and glossy finish. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile in packaging, while BoPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyester) and BoPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) are used for their enhanced barrier characteristics. Other materials include specialty films catering to niche markets.</p></p>

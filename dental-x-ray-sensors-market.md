<p><p>The dental X-ray sensors market is primarily segmented into intraoral and extraoral types. Intraoral X-rays, which are taken inside the mouth, provide detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding structures, making them essential for diagnosis and treatment planning. Extraoral X-rays, on the other hand, are captured outside the mouth and provide broader views of the facial structure and jaw, useful for assessing overall dental health, growth patterns, and orthodontic evaluations. Each type plays a crucial role in modern dentistry.</p></p>

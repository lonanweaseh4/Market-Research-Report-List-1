<p><p>The Rubber Soft Carbon Black market includes various types, notably Semi-Reinforced Carbon Black and Thermal Carbon Black. Thermal Carbon Black, on the other hand, is produced through a thermal process, offering lower reinforcing capabilities but excellent thermal stability, making it suitable for applications requiring heat resistance, such as automotive and mechanical components.</p></p>

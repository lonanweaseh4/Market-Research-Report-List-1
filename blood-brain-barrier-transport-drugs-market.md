<p><p>The Blood-Brain Barrier (BBB) Transport Drugs Market includes various transport mechanisms that facilitate drug delivery to the brain. Carrier-mediated transport involves specific proteins that escort drugs across cell membranes. Receptor-mediated transport utilizes receptors to allow selective drug entry. Absorptive-mediated transport relies on the charge properties of drugs to enable passage. Active efflux transport mechanisms, like P-glycoprotein, extrude drugs from the brain, limiting their efficacy. Other transport methods include passive diffusion and novel approaches to enhance drug delivery efficacy.</p></p>

<p><p>The urinalysis test market is primarily categorized into two types: macroscopic urinalysis and biochemical urinalysis. Macroscopic urinalysis involves the visual examination of urine, assessing its color, clarity, and odor, which helps identify possible abnormalities. Biochemical urinalysis involves chemical tests that detect substances such as proteins, glucose, and ketones, providing insights into metabolic and renal function. Together, these methods facilitate the diagnosis of various medical conditions and are essential for effective patient management in healthcare settings.</p></p>

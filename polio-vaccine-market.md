<p><strong>The Polio Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inactivated Polio Vaccine (IPV)</li><li>Oral Polio Vaccine (OPV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polio vaccine market consists primarily of two types: Inactivated Polio Vaccine (IPV) and Oral Polio Vaccine (OPV). IPV is administered via injection and contains killed virus, providing effective immunity without the risk of vaccine-derived polio. OPV, on the other hand, is given orally and contains live attenuated virus, offering the advantage of promoting herd immunity but carrying a small risk of vaccine-derived polio. Both vaccines are essential in the global effort to eradicate poliomyelitis.</p></p>

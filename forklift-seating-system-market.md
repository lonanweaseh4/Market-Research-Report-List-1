<p><strong>The Forklift Seating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Suspension Seats</li><li>Air Suspension Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Forklift Seating System market is primarily categorized into Mechanical Suspension Seats and Air Suspension Seats. Mechanical Suspension Seats use springs and dampers to absorb shocks, providing a stable ride while keeping maintenance low. In contrast, Air Suspension Seats utilize air bladders to adjust firmness and height, offering superior comfort and adaptability to different weights and driving conditions. Both types aim to enhance operator comfort and safety, ultimately improving productivity in warehouse and industrial environments.</p></p>

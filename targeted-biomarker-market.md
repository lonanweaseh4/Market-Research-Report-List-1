<p><p>The targeted biomarker market encompasses three primary types: safety biomarkers, efficacy biomarkers, and validation biomarkers. Safety biomarkers assess potential toxicity and adverse effects of treatments, ensuring patient safety. Efficacy biomarkers evaluate the therapeutic effectiveness of a drug, helping determine whether it achieves the desired clinical outcomes. Validation biomarkers are crucial for confirming the relevance and reliability of the other biomarkers, ensuring they accurately predict safety and efficacy in clinical settings. Together, these biomarkers enhance precision medicine and optimize treatment strategies.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Wafer Aligner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Aligner</li><li>Atmosphere Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer aligner market is primarily divided into two types: vacuum aligners and atmosphere aligners. Vacuum aligners utilize a vacuum mechanism to hold wafers firmly in place, ensuring high precision and alignment during the photolithography process, crucial for semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, atmosphere aligners operate in open environments, offering flexibility and ease of use but may sacrifice some precision. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, balancing between accuracy and operational efficiency in the semiconductor production industry.</p></p>

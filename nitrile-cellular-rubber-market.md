<p><strong>The Nitrile Cellular Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell Rubber</li><li>Semi-closed Cell Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrile cellular rubber market is segmented into closed cell and semi-closed cell types, each catering to diverse applications. Closed cell rubber features sealed bubbles that provide excellent resistance to moisture and air, making it ideal for insulation and sealing uses. In contrast, semi-closed cell rubber has a mix of sealed and open cells, offering improved flexibility and cushioning, making it suitable for applications requiring sound absorption and vibration dampening. Both types are widely utilized in automotive, industrial, and consumer products.</p></p>

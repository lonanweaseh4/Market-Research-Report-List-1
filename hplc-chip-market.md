<p><p>The HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) chip market is categorized into single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase chips typically utilize a single mobile phase, offering simpler designs and easier method development, which is ideal for routine analyses. In contrast, three-phase chips integrate multiple phases, allowing for more complex separations and improved resolution. This versatility makes three-phase systems suitable for advanced applications in pharmaceuticals and biochemistry, where precision and efficiency are paramount. Each type addresses specific analytical needs in various industries.</p></p>

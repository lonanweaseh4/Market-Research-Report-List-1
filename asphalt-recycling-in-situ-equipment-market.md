<p><strong>The Asphalt Recycling In-situ Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Mix Regeneration</li><li>Cold Mix Regeneration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Asphalt recycling in-situ equipment encompasses two primary market types: hot mix regeneration and cold mix regeneration. Hot mix regeneration involves recycling asphalt material using heated techniques, allowing for immediate reuse in pavement applications. This process ensures high-quality results and enhances bonding properties. In contrast, cold mix regeneration utilizes emulsified asphalt without the need for heating, making it suitable for various climates and allowing for easier handling and application. Both methods contribute to sustainable construction practices by optimizing resource use and reducing waste.</p></p>

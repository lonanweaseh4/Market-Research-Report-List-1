<p><p>The potassium chloride market is segmented into three main types: powder, crystal, and big granule. Powdered potassium chloride is fine and highly soluble, making it ideal for various applications, including fertilizers and food processing. Crystal potassium chloride is coarser, offering slower dissolution rates, suitable for use in agricultural settings. Big granule potassium chloride has larger particle sizes, which are preferred for bulk applications in agriculture, as they ensure controlled release and reduce dust during handling.</p></p>

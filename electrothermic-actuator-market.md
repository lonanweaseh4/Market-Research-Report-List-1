<p><p>The electrothermic actuator market is divided into two main types: normally closed and normally open actuators. Normally closed actuators maintain a default closed position and only open when energized, making them ideal for safety applications where fail-safe operation is crucial. Conversely, normally open actuators remain open by default and close when activated, suited for applications requiring immediate response to signals. Both types are essential in various industries for controlling fluid flow and regulating system operations efficiently.</p></p>

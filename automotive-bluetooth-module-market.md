<p><strong>The Automotive Bluetooth Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Module</li><li>Dual-mode Bluetooth Module</li><li>Classic Bluetooth Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive Bluetooth module market comprises three main types: Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) modules, dual-mode Bluetooth modules, and classic Bluetooth modules. BLE modules are energy-efficient, ideal for applications requiring low power consumption, such as vehicle tracking and health monitoring. Dual-mode modules support both BLE and classic Bluetooth, offering flexibility for various devices. Classic Bluetooth modules enable robust data streaming and connectivity for audio and infotainment systems, ideal for high-bandwidth applications in modern vehicles. Each type caters to different automotive connectivity needs.</p></p>

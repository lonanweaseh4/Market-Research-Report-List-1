<p><p>The Omega-3 concentrates market consists of three primary types: triglycerides, ethyl esters, and phospholipids. Triglyceride omega-3s are natural fats commonly found in fish oil, offering high bioavailability. Ethyl esters are a concentrated form, often used in dietary supplements, but may have lower absorption rates. Phospholipid omega-3s, primarily found in krill oil, are easily absorbed and provide additional health benefits due to their structural components. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences and applications in health products.</p></p>

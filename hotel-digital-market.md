<p><p>In the hotel digital market, there are primarily two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based systems store data on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and regular updates, benefiting hotels seeking flexibility and lower upfront costs. On-premise solutions, on the other hand, involve software installed locally on hotel servers, providing complete control over data security and customization. While on-premise can require significant initial investment, it may better suit hotels with specific regulatory or operational needs.</p></p>

<p><p>Cargo insurance encompasses three primary market types: land, marine, and air cargo insurance. Land cargo insurance protects goods transported overland via trucks or trains, covering risks like theft or damage. Marine cargo insurance covers goods shipped over water, addressing perils like sinking or storms. Air cargo insurance safeguards shipments transported by air, offering coverage against risks like loss or damage during transit. Each type caters to specific transportation modes, ensuring protection tailored to the unique challenges of cargo movement.</p></p>

<p><p>The tissue repair materials market is categorized into two primary segments: hard tissue repair materials and soft tissue repair materials. Hard tissue repair materials include products designed for bone repair and reconstruction, such as bone grafts and fixation devices. These are utilized in orthopedic and dental surgeries. Soft tissue repair materials focus on repairing or reinforcing soft tissues, including sutures, meshes, and adhesives, which are essential in procedures involving skin, ligaments, and tendons. Both types are crucial for successful surgical outcomes.</p></p>

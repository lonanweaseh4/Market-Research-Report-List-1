<p><p>The sulfosuccinate market is categorized primarily into mono-ester and di-ester sulfocsuccinate types. Mono-ester sulfocsuccinate involves one ester group attached to a sulfosuccinic acid, making it suitable for applications requiring mild surfactants, often used in personal care products. In contrast, di-ester sulfocsuccinate contains two ester groups and offers enhanced performance in emulsification and detergency, typically used in industrial cleaning and agricultural formulations. Both types cater to diverse sectors, reflecting their functional versatility and importance in various formulations.</p></p>

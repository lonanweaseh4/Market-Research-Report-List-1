<p><p>The PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) resins market can be categorized into two primary types based on their production methods: dispersion and suspension. The dispersion method involves producing fine, stable emulsions of PTFE, resulting in a material that can be easily processed into coatings and films. In contrast, the suspension method produces larger particles suitable for applications requiring bulk properties, such as sheets and rods. Both methods cater to diverse industries, driving demand for high-performance PTFE products with distinct characteristics.</p></p>

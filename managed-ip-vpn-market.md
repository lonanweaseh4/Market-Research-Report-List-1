<p><p>The Managed IP VPN market is categorized into two main types: Remote Access VPN and Site-to-Site VPN. Remote Access VPN enables individual users to securely connect to a corporate network from remote locations, facilitating mobility and flexibility. Conversely, Site-to-Site VPN establishes secure connections between multiple fixed locations, allowing seamless communication and data transfer across various offices or branches. Both types provide enhanced security, scalability, and efficient management for organizations seeking reliable, private networking solutions.</p></p>

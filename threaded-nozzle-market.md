<p><p>The threaded nozzle market is categorized into two main types: internal thread nozzles and external thread nozzles. Internal thread nozzles feature a threaded interior that allows for connection to other components, ensuring secure fittings in various systems. In contrast, external thread nozzles have threaded exteriors, designed for attachment to compatible devices or hoses. Both types serve essential roles across industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and agriculture, offering versatility in fluid delivery, irrigation, and cooling applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Vacuum Insulated Panels (VIPs) are advanced materials used in construction for thermal insulation, characterized by a core enclosed in a vacuum-sealed envelope. The primary market types include fumed silica and glass fiber. Fumed silica VIPs leverage ultra-lightweight, high-performance silica for superior insulation and reduced thickness, ideal for space-constrained applications. In contrast, glass fiber VIPs utilize woven glass fibers to enhance durability and resistance to compression, making them suitable for various building projects requiring effective thermal management.</p></p>

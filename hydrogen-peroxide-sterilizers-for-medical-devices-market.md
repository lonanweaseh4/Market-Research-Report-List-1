<p><p>Hydrogen peroxide sterilizers for medical devices are classified into two main types: vaporized hydrogen peroxide sterilizers and fogging hydrogen peroxide sterilizers. Vaporized hydrogen peroxide sterilizers use a vaporized form of hydrogen peroxide to effectively penetrate and sterilize equipment within sealed environments. In contrast, fogging hydrogen peroxide sterilizers generate a fine mist or fog, enabling sterilization of larger areas and surfaces. Both methods are effective in eliminating pathogens, ensuring high-level disinfection for medical instruments and healthcare facilities.</p></p>

<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) insurance market consists of two main types: compulsory insurance and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance includes policies mandated by law, such as third-party liability, ensuring that NEV owners are covered for damages caused to others in accidents. Optional insurance, on the other hand, allows owners to choose additional coverage, such as comprehensive or collision insurance, providing protection for their own vehicle against theft, damage, or accidents beyond the legal requirements.</p></p>

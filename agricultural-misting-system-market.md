<p><p>The agricultural misting system market is primarily divided into low pressure and high pressure systems. Low pressure misting systems operate at lower water pressure levels, often using less power and providing a gentle mist suitable for smaller farms and greenhouses. Conversely, high pressure misting systems function at elevated pressures, producing finer mist droplets that improve evaporation, cooling, and moisture retention in larger agricultural settings. Each type caters to specific agricultural needs, enhancing crop growth and managing environmental conditions effectively.</p></p>

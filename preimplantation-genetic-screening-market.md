<p><p>The Preimplantation Genetic Screening market comprises several key technologies. Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) offers comprehensive genetic analysis, allowing for the detection of numerous abnormalities. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies specific DNA sequences for targeted assessments. Fluorescent In-situ Hybridization (FISH) visualizes genetic material using fluorescent probes to identify chromosomal anomalies. Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) analyzes variations in DNA content between samples, while Single Nucleotide Polymorphism (SNP) testing identifies genetic variations at a single nucleotide level, aiding in embryo selection.</p></p>

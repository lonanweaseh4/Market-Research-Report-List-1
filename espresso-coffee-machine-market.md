<p><p>The espresso coffee machine market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require hands-on control, allowing enthusiasts to brew custom espresso by adjusting variables like pressure and grind size. Semi-automatic machines offer some automation, providing controlled water flow while still requiring user input for grinding and tamping. Fully automatic machines automate the entire brewing process, simplifying operations with features like one-touch buttons and built-in grinders, making them ideal for convenience seekers.</p></p>

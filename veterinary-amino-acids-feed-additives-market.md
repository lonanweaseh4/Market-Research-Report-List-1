<p><p>The veterinary amino acids feed additives market is segmented into essential and non-essential amino acids. Essential amino acids are vital for animal growth and development, as they cannot be synthesized by the body and must be obtained through diet. Examples include lysine and methionine. Non-essential amino acids, on the other hand, can be synthesized by the body and are important for various metabolic functions. Both types play a crucial role in improving livestock health, enhancing feed efficiency, and promoting overall productivity.</p></p>

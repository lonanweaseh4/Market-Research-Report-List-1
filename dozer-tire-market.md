<p><p>The dozer tire market consists of two primary types: radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature a steel belt design that offers enhanced durability, reduced rolling resistance, and improved traction, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, bias tires have layers of fabric that provide flexibility and stability, often resulting in a lower cost and better performance on uneven terrains. Both tire types cater to specific operational needs in construction and mining, influencing purchasing decisions based on work environments.</p></p>
